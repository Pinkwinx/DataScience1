HOLLYWOOD'S GENDER DIVIDE AND ITS EFFECT ON FILMS

EXAMINING THE GENDER OF WRITERS, PORDUCERS AND DIRECTORS WHO MAKE FILMS THAT FAIL THE BECIDEL TEST

There's this thing callled the Bechel Test. It meassures just how male-dominated our belove Netflix nights really are. To pass, films need to satisfy thre requirements:

1. I has at least two women in it

2. Who talk to each other, about

3. Something besides a man

It's a low bar, but good movies don't pass. Birdman fails. Avatar fails. Fucking Toy Story fails. On bendeltest.com , a site for crowdsourcing Bechdel Test results, about 40% of films don't pass. It's a sad state since women exist in life, like, half the timee.

Why? Rather than generalizing a sexist system. one theory is simple: filmakers, unintentionally, make movies about themselves (i.ee., write what you know). Since the most powerful producers, writers, and directors are men, male-themes permate into Hollywood's output.
